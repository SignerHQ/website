No hardware to buy

Use any spare computer, laptop, or phone as your signing device. It never needs to connect to the internet.

Keys never leave the Signer

Private keys and seeds exist only on the Signer. The Wallet app sends unsigned transactions to the Signer, receives them back signed, and broadcasts via the Node.

🔌

Your choice of connection

Connect your Signer to your Wallet app via Bluetooth, USB, local WiFi, or QR codes. The Signer never touches the internet - only the Wallet app connects to the Node.

💻

Fully open source

Every line of code is auditable. No proprietary firmware, no secure elements with hidden code, no trust required.
